Design Experience

I've been a Designer on many projects, from tabletop games to web development. I focus on user-centered design, making things intuitive and built for the target audience.
Aspera
Lead Designer
March 2024
During March of 2024, I worked with a team of 3 others to create Aspera, a tabletop roleplaying game. Over the course of 4 months, we took the game from nothing to a complete book, available online and through DriveThruRPG. I acted as the lead designer, working with the team to create and solidify core gameplay systems. I wrote, tested, and iterated on the game, refining it with a player focus during the entire process.
RESET
Producer
August 2025 - Present
RESET is a Tower Defense Roguelike game developed in C# and Godot, which I produced with a core team of three other designer/developers. I managed the team, as well as 5 additional workers across disciplines such as Art, UI Design, and Sound. I also served as Lead Developer, applying Agile Development cycles and Scrum methodology to reach production goals. I designed and developed systems, including Aspects and many parts of the Wave system.
iD Tech Camps
Instructor
Summer 2025
During the Summer of 2025, I worked at iD Tech Camps as an Instructor, teaching students Game Design in Unreal Engine. In addition to covering the Blueprint visual programming system, I taught game design fundamentals and how to focus on the user experience. Cthuleuleu
Producer
August 2025 - December 2025
